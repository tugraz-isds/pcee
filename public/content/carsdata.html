A dataset which represents different cars, each with varying specifications consists of 5 dimensions and 6 records.

  • Speed: The maximum speed of the car in kilometers per hour (km/h).
  • Fuel Efficiency: The fuel efficiency of the car in liters per 100 kilometers (L/100km).
  • Weight: The weight of the car in kilograms (kg).
  • Price: The price of the car in thousand of euros (EUR).

The cars dataset was created to allow for some interesting observations. For example, cars with lower speeds tend to have higher fuel efficiency and lower prices. Conversely, cars with higher speeds tend to be heavier, more expensive, and less fuel-efficient.
