The current parallel coordinates plot visualises a dataset with 5 dimensions and 6 records, representing different cars, each with varying specifications.

  • Speed: The maximum speed of the car in kilometers per hour (km/h).

  • Weight: The weight of the car in kilograms (kg).
  • Price: The price of the car in thousand of euros (EUR).
