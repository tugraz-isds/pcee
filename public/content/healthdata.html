Heart Health Dataset

The Heart Health Dataset is a fictitious, curated dataset representing health-related measurements from six patients. It consists of a header row, 6 rows of data (records), and 6 columns (dimensions):

  • Patient: Identifier for each patient.
  • Age: The patient's age.
  • BloodPressure: Systolic blood pressure reading (mmHg).
  • HeartRate: Resting heart rate in beats per minute (bpm).
  • BMI: Body Mass Index (indicator of body fat based on height and weight).
  • Cholesterol: Total cholesterol level in the blood (mg/dL).
