A fictitious dataset of student marks, which consists of 30 rows of data (records), and 9 columns (dimensions), including the name of the student. Each row represents one student and their marks between 0 and 100 in 8 subjects. Each dimension, apart from the first, represents one subject. The student marks dataset was deliberately curated to contain some interesting outliers and correlations. For example, students who are good in English are usually also good in German.
