Resources

The following resources were helpful in understanding parallel coordinates and, consequently, in building an explorable explainer:

  • The Plane with Parallel Coordinates, Alfred Inselberg
    https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/BF01898350
  • Parallel Coordinates: Visual Multidimensional Geometry and Its Applications, Alfred Inselberg
    https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/0387215077/
  • State of the Art of Parallel Coordinates, Julian Heinrich and Daniel Weiskopf
    https://joules.de/files/heinrich_state_2013.pdf

The references below relate to this project. It was first necessary to build a parallel coordinates chart component, which can be steered from outside over an API:

  • Steerable Parallel Coordinates in D3 (SPCD3), Romana Gruber
    https://ftp.isds.tugraz.at/pub/projects/gruber-2024-10-30-spcd3.pdf
  • Steerable Parallel Coordinates in D3 (SPCD3) - GIT, Romana Gruber
    https://github.com/tugraz-isds/spcd3
  • Explorable Explainers: Survey Paper, Philipp Drescher, Jeremias Kleinschuster, Sebastian Schreiner und Burim Vrella
    https://courses.isds.tugraz.at/ivis/surveys/ss2023/ivis-ss2023-g1-survey-explorables.pdf
